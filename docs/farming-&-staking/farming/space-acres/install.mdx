## Before You Begin

Make sure you have:

1. A computer with a reliable internet connection.
2. Enough free disk space (we'll help you determine how much later).
3. Administrator access to your computer.

What to expect: 

- Sync might take from a few hours to up to a day.
- The initial syncing and plotting process requires substantial computational resources, so you may notice that your computer runs slower for day-to-day tasks until syncing and plotting are complete.

:::note
Please note that a wallet with a Substrate address is required to run the software.<br />
To learn how to create a Substrate wallet, please visit our pages on [SubWallet (recommended)](/docs/farming-&-staking/wallets/subwallet.md) or [**`{polkadot.js}`** extension](/docs/farming-&-staking/wallets/polkadot.md).
:::

## Step 2: Set Up Space Acres

Now comes the exciting partâ€”setting up your farm! Here's what you'll see on the first screen:

1. **Subspace (Autonomys) Network Rewards Address**:
   - This is where your farming rewards will be sent.
   - It starts with **st** for **SubWallet** and with **5** for `{Polkadot.js}` extension.
   - If you don't have one, click **Create wallet** to create a wallet.

2. **Choose the folder for node**:
   - This is where Space Acres will store important data.
   - Pick a location with plenty of free space; it might be as simple as **/Downloads**.

3. **Choose the folder for farming and size of the farm**:
   - This is where your **plots** (farming data) will be stored.
   - The size determines your probability of getting a reward on the Network. In most cases, larger plots (or larger farm size) will gain you more rewards.
   - If you prefer to have a few farms (e.g. you have some spare space on different SSD drives), you can click on **Add farm** to pledge some additional storage space.

4. **Advanced configuration (optional)**:

   :::tip
   Don't worry about this unless you're an experienced user.
   :::

For a comprehensive list of prerequisites and explanations, refer to our [Getting Started Guide](/docs/farming-&-staking/farming/intro.mdx).

## Step 3: Start Farming

1. After entering your information, click **Start**.
2. Space Acres will now set up your node and connect to the Autonomys Network.
3. You'll see progress bars as it syncs data and creates your farm plots.

![space-acres-setup-2](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-2.png)

4. Once everything is ready, you'll see the main Space Acres dashboard where you can monitor your farming activity.

![space-acres-setup-3](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-3.png)

5. Initial syncing and plotting can take from a few hours to days, depending on your hardware, network speed, and specified farm size. Below are examples of the various stages you can expect.

   a. **Cache Syncing**

   ![space-acres-setup-5](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-5.png) 

   b. **Initial Plotting**

   ![space-acres-setup-6](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-6.png)

   c. **Plotting Complete, Now Farming**

   ![space-acres-setup-7](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-7.png)

6. Once syncing and plotting are complete, youâ€™ll notice a drop in CPU and RAM usage, and the dashboard will display your earned rewards and the estimated time until the next reward.

![space-acres-setup-7](/img/doc-imgs/space-acres/space-acres-setup-7.png)

You can toggle the dashboard to view brief statistics or display all plotted sectors along with their status. The color codes of sectors are explained below. By hovering over the icons on the right, you can check the number of missed challenges, your average auditing and proving performance, and other useful stats.

**Happy farming!**
