The [Autonomys Staking Portal](https://staking.autonomys.xyz) is the easiest way to stake (nominate) your AI3 to an operator and start earning rewards. It guides you through choosing an operator, entering an amount, and signing the transaction - without needing to construct extrinsics manually. <Badge variant="recommended" text="Recommended" />

If you'd rather submit the staking extrinsics yourself, see the [Stake with the Substrate Portal](/staking/substrate-portal) guide instead.

:::info Before you start: tokens must be on the consensus chain
Staking takes place on the Autonomys **consensus chain** and requires a Substrate wallet - [SubWallet](/wallets/subwallet), [Talisman](/wallets/talisman), or the [Polkadot.js extension](/wallets/polkadot). MetaMask and other EVM-only wallets are **not** supported.

If your AI3 is on Auto EVM, first [bridge it to the consensus chain using XDM](/xdm) (this takes ~1 day). Keep a small amount of AI3 unstaked to cover transaction fees.
:::

## How staking works

A few things worth knowing before you stake:

- **Storage fund reserve.** When you stake, 80% of your deposit becomes your stake and 20% is reserved in the operator's storage fund, which pays for the data bundles the operator produces. The reserve is **not lost** - it is refunded proportionally when you withdraw.
- **Epochs.** Staking operations are processed in epochs (every 100 domain blocks, roughly 10 minutes). Your deposit sits in a pending queue until the next epoch transition, when it converts to shares in the operator's nomination pool and starts earning.
- **Rewards compound automatically.** There is nothing to claim while you remain staked - your share of the operator's earnings (after the operator's nomination tax) accrues to the value of your position and is paid out when you withdraw.
- **Nomination tax.** The nomination tax is the commission an operator takes from rewards before distributing the remainder to its nominators, shown as a percentage on each operator card.

## Staking

Follow these steps to stake (nominate) your AI3 to an operator and start earning rewards.

### Step 1: Connect your wallet

Go to [staking.autonomys.xyz](https://staking.autonomys.xyz) and click **Connect Wallet** in the top right. Choose your Substrate wallet extension (SubWallet, Talisman, or Polkadot.js), approve the connection request in the extension popup, and select the account holding your AI3.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Connect Wallet</summary>

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '20px', flexWrap: 'wrap'}}>

![Connect wallet](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-connect-wallet.png)

![Wallet selection modal](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-wallet-modal.png)

</div>

</details>

### Step 2: Browse the operators list

Open the **Operators** page using the **Staking** link in the top navigation. Each operator shows its nomination tax, estimated APY, total staked value, nominator count, and status. Use the search box or switch between grid and table views to compare operators.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Operators List</summary>

![Operators list](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-operators-list.png)

</details>

### Step 3: Choose an operator and click "Stake"

Click **Stake** on the operator you want to nominate. You'll be taken to that operator's staking page (headed "Stake to" followed by the operator's name), which shows a summary of the operator alongside the staking form.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Stake Page</summary>

![Stake to operator page](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-stake-page.png)

</details>

### Step 4: Enter the amount to stake

Enter the amount of AI3 in the **Amount to Stake** field, or click **MAX** to stake your full available balance (a small buffer is kept back for transaction fees). The form shows the operator's minimum stake, which applies to your **first** stake only.

The Transaction Breakdown shows how your deposit is split: 80% becomes your stake and 20% is reserved in the operator's storage fund (refunded proportionally when you withdraw).

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Amount &amp; Transaction Breakdown</summary>

![Amount to stake form](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-amount-form.png)

</details>

### Step 5: Submit and sign the transaction

Click **Stake Tokens**. Your wallet extension will pop up asking you to sign the transaction - review the details and approve. The button shows *Awaiting signature...* and then *Submitting...* while the transaction is broadcast.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Sign Transaction</summary>

![Sign the staking transaction](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/staking-sign-transaction.png)

</details>

### Step 6: Confirmation and epoch activation

On success you'll see a **Staking Successful!** screen. Your stake appears as **Pending** on your Dashboard until the next epoch transition (~10 minutes), after which it becomes active and starts earning rewards. Rewards are automatically compounded - there is nothing to claim while you remain staked.

## Unstaking and withdrawing

Withdrawing your staked AI3 is a two-step process: request the withdrawal, then claim your funds after a locking period of roughly one day.

### Step 1: Open your Dashboard and click "Withdraw"

Go to the [Dashboard](https://staking.autonomys.xyz/dashboard) with your wallet connected, find the position you want to exit in the **Active Positions** table, and click **Withdraw**.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Dashboard Withdraw</summary>

![Dashboard withdraw button](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-dashboard-withdraw.png)

</details>

### Step 2: Choose partial or full withdrawal

On the withdrawal page for that operator, choose a withdrawal method: **Partial** (enter the total amount you want to receive) or **Full Withdrawal**.

:::caution Partial withdrawals and the minimum stake
If a partial withdrawal would leave your remaining stake below the operator's minimum, the portal will warn you and convert it into a full withdrawal of your entire position.
:::

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Withdrawal Method</summary>

![Partial or full withdrawal](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-method.png)

</details>

### Step 3: Review the summary, submit and sign

The **Withdrawal Summary** shows the amount you'll receive - including your proportional storage fund refund - and the transaction fee. Click **Withdraw Tokens** and sign the transaction in your wallet extension. On success you'll see a **Withdrawal Successful!** confirmation. Withdrawal requests are processed at the end of the current epoch.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Summary &amp; Confirmation</summary>

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '20px', flexWrap: 'wrap'}}>

![Withdrawal summary](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-summary.png)

![Withdrawal successful](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-withdrawal-successful.png)

</div>

</details>

### Step 4: Wait for the locking period

Withdrawn funds have a locking period of 14,400 domain blocks (~1 day) before they can be claimed. Track progress in the **Pending Operations** card on your Dashboard: the withdrawal shows as **Withdrawing** with a countdown (for example, *Ready in 23 hours 59 minutes*), then changes to **Unlockable** when ready.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Pending Operations</summary>

![Pending operations countdown](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-pending-operations.png)

</details>

### Step 5: Claim your funds

Once your withdrawal shows as **Unlockable**, a summary appears with the total amount ready. Click **Claim All** and sign the transaction. Your AI3 - including the storage fund refund - is returned to your wallet's available balance.

<details>
<summary><Icon icon="mdi:image-multiple" /> Claim Funds</summary>

<div style={{display: 'flex', gap: '20px', flexWrap: 'wrap'}}>

![Claim all](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-claim-all.png)

![Funds claimed successfully](/img/doc-imgs/staking-portal/unstake-claimed-success.png)

</div>

</details>

:::tip Withdrawal timing at a glance
1. **Request** the withdrawal - processed at the end of the current epoch (~10 minutes).
2. **Wait** the locking period - 14,400 domain blocks (~1 day).
3. **Claim** your funds with the **Claim All** button on the Dashboard.

In total, expect roughly one day between requesting a withdrawal and having the funds back in your wallet.
:::
