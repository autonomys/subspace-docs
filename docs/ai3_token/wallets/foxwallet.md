:::note
FoxWallet is a third-party multi-chain mobile wallet created by the [FoxWallet Team](https://foxwallet.com/).

_Although FoxWallet maintains a browser extension, it does not natively support the Autonomys Network framework. This guide details the mobile application configuration workflow exclusively._
:::

FoxWallet provides a decentralized mobile infrastructure optimized for multi-chain asset tracking and secure transaction handling within the Web3 ecosystem.

---

## Installation of Wallet

1. Visit the official [FoxWallet download page](https://foxwallet.com/) on your mobile device and install the appropriate app store version for your operating system (iOS or Android).

![step-1](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-1.jpg)

2. Launch the application. You will be prompted with primary onboarding selections to either initialize a fresh configuration or pull in an existing account matrix.

![step-2](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-3.jpg)

## Managing Your Accounts

To maintain clean accounting parameters before interfacing with network validation pipelines, choose the configuration path that matches your current cryptographic profile.

### Creating a New Account (Recommended for Staking)

1. Select **Create wallet** and check the confirmation box to agree to the terms of service.

![step-3](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-2.jpg)

2. Provide a safe local wallet name and define a strong security master password for local device validation. Click **Confirm**.

![step-4](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-4.jpg)

3. The system will advance you to the cryptographic backup layer. A security acknowledgement screen will display regarding data exposure rules. Review the safety parameters and select **Confirm backup**.

:::warning Protect Your Cryptocurrency Recovery Phrase at All Costs
Your recovery phrase (also known as a seed phrase) is the master key to your cryptocurrency wallet. Anyone with access to this phrase can take complete control of your funds. Never share it with anyone, never store it in unsecured digital platforms or emails, and always ensure you have a safe offline backup. Treat it like your most sensitive personal or financial information. _If it’s exposed or lost, you could permanently lose access to your cryptocurrency_.
:::

4. Your unique 12-word recovery sequence will be displayed. Write down the seed phrase manually onto a physical sheet.

   _Note: The application explicitly blocks local device screenshots on this interface to avoid background cloud storage parsing risks._

5. Verify your backup records by selecting the word strings in their exact chronological indexing sequence and click **Yes**.

6. Upon successful sequence validation, you will be redirected to the primary multi-chain asset dashboard view.

![step-5](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-5.jpg)

### Importing an Existing Wallet

1. Select **Import wallet** from the onboarding home screen and accept the terms of service.

![step-6](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-2.jpg)

2. Manually input your pre-existing 12-word mnemonic recovery phrase into the primary field matrix and click **Next step**.

![step-7](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-6.jpg)

3. Set up and confirm your local application access password configuration.

![step-8](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-7.jpg)

4. Finalize the initialization sequence to access your wallet profile parameters directly on the primary mobile interface workspace.

![step-8](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-5.jpg)

## Backing Up Your Wallet

If you need to retrieve your seed phrase elements later to verify your offline storage assets or clone your configuration profiles across hardware devices:

1. Select the **Me** profile navigation tab icon located at the bottom right corner of the primary application view.

![step-9](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-8.png)

2. Within the primary configuration options menu layout, select **Accounts**.

![step-10](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-9.jpg)

3. The interface will display your active localized wallet setups.

![step-11](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-10.png)

4. Identify your target profile asset row and select the **three vertical dots** contextual configurations menu icon in the top right corner.

![step-12](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-11.png)

5. Select **Export seed phrase** from the operational properties selection slider.

![step-13](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-12.png)

6. Provide your wallet validation profile password, acknowledge the dynamic image data warning, and select **View seed phrase**.

![step-14](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-13.png)

7. Safely audit your exposed keys and record any missing information items.

![step-14](/img/doc-imgs/foxwallet/FoxWallet-14.jpg)

8. Select **Confirm backup** to reset your security layout context and return safely to your standard home screen workspace.
