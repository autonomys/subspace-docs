:::note
SubWallet is a third-party wallet built for Substrate and EVM networks by the [SubWallet Team](https://subwallet.app).

_This guide covers the browser extension setup. A mobile app is also available on [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.subwallet.mobile) and [iOS](https://testflight.apple.com/join/ZW3pUbWj)._
:::

SubWallet is a user-friendly Web3 Multiverse Gateway for the Substrate ecosystem designed to provide a secure and streamlined way to connect to blockchain-based applications.

To learn more about SubWallet, visit their official [Website](https://subwallet.app).

---

## Create or Setup Your SubWallet

1. Visit the [SubWallet download page](https://www.subwallet.app/download.html) and install the respective extension version for your browser.
2. Once the extension is installed, open it from your browser's toolbar.
3. The onboarding interface will display several setup options. To initiate a secure staking setup, select **Create a new account**.

:::tip Existing Wallets
If you prefer to import an active pre-existing keyphrase instead of generating a fresh address, select **Import an account**.
:::

![step-3](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-1.png)

4. Carefully read and accept the SubWallet Terms of Use.

![step-4](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-2.png)

5. Enter and confirm a strong, master extension password.

![step-5](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-3.png)

6. Review the structural safety information explaining how to keep your private keys secure. Click on each safety tip to acknowledge that you understand it.

![step-6](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-4.png)

## Managing Your Accounts

To maintain strict accounting precision while staking on the Autonomys Network, it is ideal to generate a fresh target account.

### Creating a New Account (Recommended for Staking)

1. Provide an easily recognizable local name for your fresh account (e.g., "Autonomys Staking Pool").

![step-7](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-5.png)

2. Your unique 12-word seed phrase will be generated. Write it down carefully in physical format.

![step-8](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-6.png)

:::warning Protect Your Cryptocurrency Recovery Phrase at All Costs
Your recovery phrase (also known as a seed phrase) is the master key to your cryptocurrency wallet. Anyone with access to this phrase can take complete control of your funds. Never share it with anyone, never store it in unsecured digital platforms or emails, and always ensure you have a safe offline backup. Treat it like your most sensitive personal or financial information. _If it’s exposed or lost, you could permanently lose access to your cryptocurrency_.
:::

3. Confirm that you have backed up the phrase securely. You will then see the deployment success screen.

![step-9](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-7.png)

## Connect to Autonomys & Find Your Public Address

Follow these steps to activate the native Autonomys asset display tracking and grab your public address.

1. Open the main wallet screen, scroll to the bottom, and click on **Manage tokens**.

![wallet-1](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-8.png)

2. For clarity, you may deselect all automatically enabled tokens belonging to foreign networks.

![wallet-2](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-9.png)

3. In the search box, filter for `Autonomys`. Toggle on the network visibility switches required for your workflow (e.g., **mainnet** or **Chronos** testnet options).

![wallet-3](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-10.png)

4. Return to the primary screen where your active **AI3** asset balances are listed. Click directly on the asset name row.

![wallet-4](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-11.png)

5. Click the dedicated **Copy** icon button to send your consensus public address straight to your system clipboard.

![wallet-5](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Subwallet-12.png)

## Connect to Autonomys Network via RPC

If an active development network or specific custom testnet endpoint is not appearing inside the default token list, you can map your provider node endpoints manually. Use the current RPC network strings provided below:

<Tabs>
  <TabItem value="mainnet" label="Mainnet" default>

    ### Consensus
    ```
    wss://rpc.mainnet.subspace.foundation/ws
    ```

  </TabItem>
  
  <TabItem value="chronos" label="Chronos Testnet">

    ### Consensus
    ```
    wss://rpc.chronos.autonomys.xyz/ws
    ```

    ### Auto EVM
    ```
    wss://auto-evm.chronos.autonomys.xyz/ws
    ```

  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

1. Open the wallet dashboard interface and select the three-line configuration menu icon in the top left corner.

   ![rpc-step-1](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/rpc-1.png)

2. Inside the settings panel, select the **Manage Networks** option.

   ![rpc-step-2](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/rpc-2.png)

3. Click on the **+** (Add) icon located in the top right corner of the menu.

   ![rpc-step-3](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/rpc-3.png)

4. Populate the **Provider URL** field with one of the valid active network strings listed above. The interface will automatically query the node endpoint to map the remaining parameters (Network Name, Symbol, and default Subscan tracking explorer).

   _Note: The default block explorer for the network is the [Subscan Block Explorer](https://autonomys.subscan.io)._

   ![rpc-step-4](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/rpc-4.png)

5. Click **Save** to lock in the endpoint configurations, then navigate back to your standard network list to switch your workspace focus to the new node.

   ![rpc-step-5](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/rpc-5.png)

## Backing Up Your Wallet

Maintaining alternative offline exports of your account profile ensures long-term recovery capabilities.

1. Click directly on your active **Account avatar name banner** at the top of the interface.

   ![trouble-1](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Trouble-1.png)

2. Identify the specific wallet profile requiring an export and click its corresponding **pencil/edit icon**.

   ![trouble-2](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Trouble-2.png)

3. Select the **Export** option from the operational dropdown actions list.

   ![trouble-3](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Trouble-3.png)

4. Provide your master confirmation password. Choose your preferred structural export strategy (**Seed phrase**, backup **JSON** file, or scannable **QR code**) and follow the on-screen prompts to download or record the backup safely.

   ![trouble-4](/img/doc-imgs/subwallet/Trouble-4.png)
