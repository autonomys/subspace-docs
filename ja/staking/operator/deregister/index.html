Deregister an Operator

Operator deregistration‚Äã

To deregister an operator on the domain and have your tokens released:

info

Only account who registered an operator can deregister it. Make sure to use the same wallet / account to sign the transaction for deregistration.

Operator deregistration using Autonomys Staking interface‚Äã

  1. Proceed to the staking tab on Astral and connect your wallet.

  1. Select the wallet you would like to connect. Make sure to select the wallet you registered your operator with. SubWallet, NovaWallet, and Talisman wallets are supported.

  1. Enter your password to give an access to your wallet.

  1. Click on Operators and then toggle the filter from All operators to My nominations. This will allow you to easily find your operator(s) in the list.

  1. Click on the red X button next to an operator you would like to deregister and confirm you want to deregister.

  1. Approve the request in the pop-up window.

  1. Congratulations, your operator was deregistered.
info

It can take up to 10 minutes for the operator to be deregistered, but the funds will stay locked for 14,400 blocks, or 24 hours.

Unlocking Funds‚Äã

info

Once 14,400 domain blocks have passed after deregistering your operator your funds can now be unlocked.

  1. Follow steps 1 to 3 in the deregistration steps to connect and access your wallet.

  2. Your funds will be automatically withdrawn after 14,400 domain blocks (can be from 1-2 days depending on block production speed).

  1. Click on the Unlock Funds under the operator you would like to unlock funds from. If that button is not available, then 14,400 domain blocks have not passed.

  1. Approve the request in the pop-up window.

  1. Congratulations, your operator funds should be unlocked within the next 10 minutes.
