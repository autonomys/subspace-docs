FoxWallet

note

FoxWallet is a 3rd party multi-chain wallet created by the FoxWallet Team. Although FoxWallet has a browser extension, it does not yet support Autonomys. This guide deals with the mobile app only.

Installation of Wallet​

  1. Visit the FoxWallet website on your device and download the appropriate version for your device.

  1. You will be presented with the options to create or import a wallet.

Create a new wallet​

  1. Select Create wallet and you will need to agree with the Terms of Service.

  1. At Step 1 of the wallet creation process, you will need to select a password for the wallet and select Confirm.

  1. After confirming, you will go to Step 2 where you can see your seed phrase. You will first need to acknowledge a warning about protecting the phrase.

  2. This screen is set to prevent screenshots for safety reasons. To advance to step 3, you will need to Confirm backup.

Protect Your Cryptocurrency Recovery Phrase at All Costs

Your recovery phrase (also known as a seed phrase) is the master key to your cryptocurrency wallet. Anyone with access to this phrase can take complete control of your funds. Never share it with anyone, never store it in unsecured digital platforms or emails, and always ensure you have a safe offline backup. Treat it like your most sensitive personal or financial information. If it’s exposed or lost, you could permanently lose access to your cryptocurrency.

  1. Confirming that backup will require you to click on each word phrase in the correct order and then select Yes.

  2. Congratulations, you are now on the home page where you can see your assets and create transactions.

Import an existing wallet​

  1. Select Import wallet and you will need to agree with the Terms of Service.

  1. At Step 1 of the wallet import process, enter your existing seed phrase.

  1. After entering your seed phrase and clicking Next step, you will advance to Step 2 where you will need to set a password for the wallet.

  1. Clicking next step will take you to the home page where you can see your assets and create transactions.

Troubleshooting​

If you face any trouble or would like to learn about other features for FoxWallet, please see the Official FoxWallet Documentation.

How do I backup my wallet?​

  1. You can backup/export your wallet. Select the Me icon on the bottom right of the application.

  1. You will be presented with a list of options. Select Accounts to continue.

  1. Next you will see any accounts that you have created or imported.

  1. Click the three dots in the upper right hand corner.

  1. Then click on the first option Export seed phrase.

  1. Acknowledge the warning about not taking a screenshot and click View seed phrase.

  1. Your seed phrase will be displayed.

  1. Once you Confirm backup, you will be returned to the home page.
